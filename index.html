About the Workshop

The evolution towards Industry 5.0 is driven by intelligent, autonomous, and collaborative industrial systems. At the core of this transformation lies the challenge of enabling seamless collaboration not just within a single factory but across entire multi-enterprise ecosystems. This workshop focuses on the critical role of Service-Oriented Computing (SOC) in realising industrial-scale intelligence and collaboration. We aim to explore the foundational models, methods, and technologies for building dynamic and trustworthy industrial service ecosystems.

Topics of Interest

  • Service-Oriented Models for Enterprise Collaboration
  • Trusted Data Sharing Services
  • Intelligent Agents in Industry
  • Service-Based Digital Twins
  • Edge-Cloud Service Computing
  • Industrial Service Ecosystems
  • Human-in-the-Loop Service Systems

…and other topics related to service intelligence for collaborative industry.
